Emote Mod adds four simple emotes that appear above the Jump King character.

Controls:

Shift + Up: Happy
Shift + Left: Sad
Shift + Right: Thinking
Shift + Down: Angry

The selected emote is displayed for about two seconds.

The mod creates four editable PNG files next to the mod. Existing files are not overwritten, so you can replace them with your own images and restart the game.

GitHub: https://github.com/eski4869/EmoteMod
